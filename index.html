      S1-4 • Memorization vs generalization
      
More data closes the train–test gap

      
Over-parameterized network on a noisy 2D task. Train with 20, 200, or 2000 samples and watch the gap shrink.

      

        

          
          

            
            
          

        

        

          
Results

      Session 1 takeaway
      
What this proves

      

        

          
Architecture

          
Nonlinear activations unlock curved boundaries. Depth without nonlinearity collapses to a single linear map.
             Embeddings organize by prediction structure, not explicit similarity labels.

        

        

          
Data

          
High capacity + tiny data = memorization. Scale data and the model must learn the real pattern.
             This is the foundation for why LLM training needs massive, clean text.
